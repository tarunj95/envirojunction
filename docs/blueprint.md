# **App Name**: EnviroConnect

## Core Features:

- Environmental News Feed: Aggregates and displays current events related to climate change and environmental issues from various global, national, and regional sources.
- Environmental Job Board: Lists internships and job opportunities in waste management, clean energy, green architecture, and other related environmental sectors, specifically focused on opportunities within India.
- Tender Notifications: Provides access to domestic and global tenders and bids, enabling private firms and NGOs to find opportunities with government and international organizations. An AI tool will scan all posted tenders to assist the user in determining relevance.
- Professional Profile: Allows environmental professionals to register and create profiles to showcase their skills and availability for freelance work.
- Freelance Opportunity Board: Connects registered professionals with freelance opportunities, displaying projects that match their skills and interests.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Forest Green (#386641) to represent nature and sustainability.
- Background color: Light Beige (#FAF8F1) for a clean and natural feel.
- Accent color: Earth Orange (#D68438) to highlight calls to action and important information.
- Body and headline font: 'PT Sans', a humanist sans-serif, to balance a modern look with warmth.
- Use minimalist icons with a focus on environmental symbols, such as leaves, water droplets, and renewable energy sources.
- A clean and intuitive layout, emphasizing readability and easy navigation through job listings, news, and tender opportunities.
- Subtle transitions and loading animations to create a smooth user experience.
